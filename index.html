Projects

Tailored Knits

I love modifying, customizing, and creating sweaters for myself, friends, and family. Creating a well-fitting garment requires keeping track of measurement and fabric details, adjusting as you go. In the knitting online space there is currently not a site that can be used for modifying patterns and tracking these details. I wanted to demonstrate the use of React and create a tool I can use and share with the knitting community.

Technologies: React, Redux, Thunk, Redux-Form, Enzyme, Node, Express, MongoDB, AWS S3, Mongoose, Passport, JWT, Heroku, Travis, mLab, REST APIs, Mocha, Chai, Javascript, JQuery, CSS, HTML

Postcard

I wanted to demonstrate the use of back-end technologies and have fun. I decided to build the social media site I want where users can share photos with captions that doesn't encourage advertising, politics, or egos.

Northwest Construction Parts

A website for Northwest Construction Parts, a business that sells heavy equipment replacement parts in the Four Corners region and on eBay. This website integrates the eBay search function, so users can search their inventory and be directed to eBay to purchase items.
